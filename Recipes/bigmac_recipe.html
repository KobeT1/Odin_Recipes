THE BIGMAC

The Big Mac is an iconic and mouthwatering sandwich offered by McDonald's. It features two all-beef patties, special sauce, lettuce, cheese slices, pickles, onions, and a sesame seed bun.

Ingredients:

  • 2x All beef patties
  • Special sauce
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese slices (usually American cheese)
  • Pickles
  • Onions (diced or dehydrated)
  • Sesame seed bun

Steps:

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Preheat a grill or stovetop skillet to medium-high heat.
  3. Season the beef patties with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  5. Prepare the special sauce by combining mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, sweet pickle relish, finely chopped onions, and a touch of vinegar in a bowl.
  6. Lightly toast the sesame seed buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble the Big Mac: spread the special sauce on the bottom bun, layer lettuce, place a patty and add cheese, pickles, and onions.
  8. Repeat the previous step with the remaining patty, cheese, pickles, and onions.
  9. Spread another layer of the special sauce on the top bun and place it on top of the assembled ingredients.
  10. Gently press the burger to bring all the flavors together.

Your homemade Big Mac is now ready to be enjoyed! Serve it with some crispy French fries and your favorite beverage for the ultimate fast food experience.
